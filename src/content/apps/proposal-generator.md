## Intended delivery

Proposal Generator is a planned workflow for turning approved CRM, discovery, offer, and proof sources into a reviewable proposal draft. The hosted, custom-build, done-for-you, and licensed delivery modes are intended packaging, not current availability.

Scope, pricing, claims, terms, and next steps would remain visible for accountable human review and approval. Missing or conflicting source material would stop the draft from being treated as authoritative; the workflow would not invent commercial terms.

Versioning and buyer-engagement signals are intended to support follow-up, while acceptance and fulfillment handoff would require approved state. Signature and payment integrations are planned interfaces, not available capabilities or evidence of a closed deal.
