AI Setter is a planned appointment-setting product for inbound and approved
outbound motions. It is designed to carry context from qualification into the
conversation, propose valid times, confirm expectations, and leave a usable
handoff for the next owner.

The catalogue does not claim that messages, calendars, or bookings are live. A
queued outreach job is not a delivered message, and a calendar event is not an
attended meeting. The Autonomous Business suite keeps those states separate.
