AI Dialer is a planned voice-sales product. Its operating contract must include
consent and do-not-call checks, permitted calling windows, identity disclosure,
recording rules, a versioned script, transfer behavior, and exact stop
conditions before a call can be attempted.

No telephony route, compliance posture, or conversion result is claimed live by
this catalogue. The dialer is a separately sellable part of the Autonomous
Business suite, not permission for unbounded automated calling.
