Hermes Financial Adviser is a planned financial intelligence and decision-support
product. It should help an operator research, monitor, compare, and document
financial choices; it should not be marketed as a substitute for a licensed
human adviser or as a promise of returns. Any future execution or personalized
recommendation surface needs its own suitability, jurisdiction, disclosure,
and human-review proof.
