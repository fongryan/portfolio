AI Qualifier is a planned inbound and outbound qualification product. It turns
the seller's real acceptance criteria into a consistent conversation and a
traceable disposition. The agent should ask only for information needed to make
the next decision, explain missing evidence, and escalate edge cases.

The product does not claim live qualification results or permission to contact
prospects today. It is the decision boundary between lead generation and
setting in the Autonomous Business suite, and it can also be sold on its own.

Outreach is limited to authorized contacts and enforces opt-out requests. The
buyer owns a non-discriminatory qualification rubric. The agent will not fabricate
evidence when information is missing.
