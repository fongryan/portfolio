Autonomous Business is the planned flagship for founders who want a company that
keeps moving without making the founder the router for every task. It combines
durable company context, specialized Hermes agents, capability limits, approval
gates, execution receipts, outcome tracking, and a learning loop.

The product does not claim that unrestricted company autonomy or general access
is live today. The intended standard is stronger than “an AI completed a task”:
the operator should be able to see why work started, what authority it used,
what changed in the world, what it cost, and whether the business outcome
improved. Read the [public Autonomous Business suite operating
thesis](https://github.com/fongryan/portfolio/blob/main/docs/autonomous-business-suite.md).
