Armalo App is the flagship and the wedge. It is a multiplayer workspace —
several people, one AI, one growing conversation — where a team describes what
it wants and watches it get built on a shared canvas. There is no demo video
and no waitlist: visiting [app.armalo.ai](https://app.armalo.ai) drops you
into a live workspace immediately.

That instant, shareable surface is the strategy. Consumer products spread by
being handed around; enterprise products get bought after they are already
inside the building. Armalo App is built to do both in order: a builder tries
it alone, pulls two teammates into the thread, the team outgrows the free
tier, and the company that now depends on it asks for the dedicated,
self-hosted version. The wedge runs from a free workspace to Team seats to an
enterprise boundary — same product, same thread, deeper commitment at each
step. The full thesis is documented in `docs/consumer-wedge-to-enterprise.md`
in this site's repository.

Every other entry in this catalogue is downstream of this one: Armalo builds
and operates those products inside Armalo App itself. The catalogue is the
proof of work; the app is the machine that produces it.

Honest status: the public workspace, pricing, and paid plans are live; the
flywheel stage is [launch](/flywheel) — earning its first users — and will
only advance to monetize and compound as verified revenue lands, per this
site's proof rules.
