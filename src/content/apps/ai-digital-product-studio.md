AI Digital Product Studio is a planned workflow for experts with useful source
material and a reachable audience. It can help synthesize an offer, draft the
learning or service journey, prepare launch assets, and operate delivery—but the
expert remains responsible for truth, quality, promises, and audience trust.

The studio works only from owned or licensed source material and will not
impersonate the expert. The catalogue does not claim a profitable launch,
proprietary audience access, or permission to reuse someone else's work. Demand
remains a hypothesis until tested. The first proof should be a small manual or
assisted pilot with real users, measured outcomes, refund visibility, and
evidence that the product deserves a larger launch.
