AI Lead Generation is a planned prospect-research product. It is intended to
discover accounts through permitted sources, enrich only the fields the buyer
needs, score fit against a named rubric, and preserve evidence for review. It
should reject weak or disallowed sources instead of manufacturing confidence.

The catalogue does not claim a live data-provider connection, a guaranteed
volume of leads, or permission to scrape any source. This is one modular product
in the Autonomous Business suite; a customer can buy it before adopting the
full company operating system.
