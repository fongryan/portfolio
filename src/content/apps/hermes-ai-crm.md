Hermes AI CRM is a planned product family for agent-assisted revenue operations.
Its intended center is a canonical lead and opportunity record: what is known,
what the agent proposed, what a human approved, what was actually delivered,
and what happened next. The catalogue entry is a product description, not a
claim that every integration or reporting surface is generally available.
