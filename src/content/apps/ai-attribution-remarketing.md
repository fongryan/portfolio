AI Attribution & Remarketing is a planned product family for teams that need a
clearer line from paid attention to qualified pipeline, purchases, retention,
and long-term customer value. Its job is to reconcile evidence and make the
next growth decision easier—not to promise perfect identity or causality.

It is the campaign-activation module of the broader [Revenue Intelligence
Platform](/apps/revenue-intelligence-platform): the platform owns consented
event measurement and proof classes; this module turns approved findings into
audience and follow-up recommendations.

HYROS is a category reference for combining attribution data, ad optimization,
and AI remarketing. This Armalo entry is not affiliated with HYROS and does not
copy its product, data, integrations, or performance claims. A real deployment
requires consent, identity resolution, source coverage, conversion definitions,
incrementality checks, and customer-specific measurement proof.
