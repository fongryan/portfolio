AI Salesman is the planned full-cycle member of the revenue workforce. It can
coordinate discovery, qualification, follow-up, proposal preparation, and an
approved closing path without losing the evidence accumulated by earlier
agents. “Full-cycle” does not mean unlimited authority.

The product does not claim a live autonomous closer or guaranteed revenue. The
Autonomous Business suite requires authoritative product facts, explicit
commercial limits, approval gates for novel commitments, delivery evidence,
and a human owner for exceptions.
