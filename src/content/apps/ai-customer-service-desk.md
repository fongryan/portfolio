AI Customer Service Desk is the planned parent bundle for Armalo's separately
buyable email, SMS, voice, and WhatsApp customer-service modules. A buyer can
start with one channel when the workflow is narrow, then add channels without
rebuilding policy, knowledge, customer identity, routing, or reporting.

The catalogue does not claim live inbox, telephony, or messaging access. Every
implementation needs explicit data sources, response policy, channel consent,
identity, escalation, delivery receipts, retention rules, and a human owner for
exceptions before it can handle customer demand.
