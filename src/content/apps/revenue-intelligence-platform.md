The platform uses first-party or consented data. Attribution is directional
evidence, not a causal claim; incrementality requires an appropriate experiment.
There are no autonomous budget changes, and any such change requires approval.

This is the parent measurement layer. [AI Attribution &
Remarketing](/apps/ai-attribution-remarketing) is its narrower campaign-activation
module, not a second canonical attribution platform.
